Our mission

Ask Me For Water is a community‑driven initiative built on compassion and unity. We create a public directory of individuals and organizations who are willing to share clean drinking water with anyone who asks. By connecting people and spreading awareness, we hope to ensure that everyone has access to one of life’s most fundamental necessities.
